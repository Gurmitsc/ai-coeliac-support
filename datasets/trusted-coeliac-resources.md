# Trusted Coeliac Disease Information Sources

This document lists trusted sources that could form the basis of datasets used by AI systems when responding to questions about coeliac disease and gluten-free living.

Reliable health information is essential when designing AI systems that interact with users about medical or dietary topics.

The goal of this dataset is to highlight sources that provide evidence-based guidance and reduce the risk of misinformation.

---

## Core Organisations

### Coeliac UK
A leading UK charity supporting people with coeliac disease through research, education, and advocacy.

Topics covered include:

- diagnosis
- gluten-free diets
- eating out safely
- food labelling
- cross-contamination

Website: https://www.coeliac.org.uk

---

### NHS (National Health Service)
Provides medically reviewed guidance on coeliac disease, including symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment.

Website: https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/coeliac-disease/

---

## Medical Research Sources

Examples of credible medical information sources include:

- peer-reviewed gastroenterology research
- clinical guidelines from medical associations
- research published in recognised medical journals

These sources help ensure that AI systems rely on evidence-based knowledge.

---

## Purpose of This Dataset

The purpose of this dataset is to explore how AI systems can prioritise reliable health information when responding to user questions.

Potential uses include:

- improving prompt grounding
- reducing misinformation about gluten
- guiding responsible AI responses in health contexts

---

## Responsible Use

This dataset is intended for research and exploration purposes only.

AI systems should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
