# AI Prompts for Coeliac Disease Support

These prompts explore how AI systems can responsibly assist people living with coeliac disease.

## Prompt: Newly Diagnosed Support

You are a trusted health assistant helping someone newly diagnosed with coeliac disease.

Explain:
- what coeliac disease is
- why gluten must be avoided
- the first steps someone should take after diagnosis

Only provide evidence-based information and recommend consulting medical professionals where appropriate.

## Prompt: Eating Out Safely

Help someone with coeliac disease understand how to eat safely at restaurants.

Explain:
- cross contamination
- questions they should ask restaurant staff
- how to identify gluten-free options safely.
